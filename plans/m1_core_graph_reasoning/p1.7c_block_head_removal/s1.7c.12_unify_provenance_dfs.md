## Goal

The provenance-chain depth-first walk is written twice — `back_prop.py:94`
`_walk` and `commitment.py:198` `_reaches_commitment` — differing **only in
the terminal test** (does this fact reach a hypothesis vs reach a specific
commitment). Same visited-set bookkeeping, same ancestor recursion.

## Tasks

- Extract one `_reaches(kb, fact, visited, is_terminal) -> bool` (or a
  generator `_walk(kb, fact, visited)` the callers filter) into a shared
  provenance module.
- `back_prop` passes its hypothesis-terminal predicate; `commitment` passes
  its commitment-terminal predicate.
- Delete both local DFS bodies.

## Acceptance

- One provenance DFS; both callers pass a predicate.
- Back-prop + commitment tests green (these exercise the two terminal cases);
  full suite green; verdicts byte-identical on the 3-variant gate.
