# S1.7c.16 — Factor a `_BaseStats`

**Phase:** P1.7c, Track B.
**Estimate:** ~1 hr. Low risk.
**Depends on:** P1.7b baseline. Pairs naturally with
[S1.7c.15](s1.7c.15_split_lattice_loop_state.md).
**Finding:** [F-ENG-9](../p1.7b_review_and_refactor/findings.md#eng--monotonic-search-engine-inferencemonotonic).

## Goal

`MonotonicStats` (`solver.py:132`) and `LatticeStats` (`lattice.py:55`) share
**11 byte-identical counter fields**, and `_build_lattice_stats`
(`solver.py:464`) copies them across by hand — a list that silently goes stale
when a counter is added to one but not the other.

## Tasks

- Lift the 11 shared counters into a `_BaseStats` dataclass; have both stats
  types inherit and add only their entry-specific extras.
- Replace the hand copy in `_build_lattice_stats` with field inheritance / a
  single `replace`-style construction.

## Acceptance

- Every stats counter value is byte-identical to baseline on the 3-variant
  gate.
- Full suite green.
